01

Brand Identity

Brand Name

SHIFO

"Shifo" means "healing" in Tajik/Uzbek, reflecting the core mission of the platform: to bring modern digital solutions to healthcare management across Central Asia.

Mission Statement

To empower medical clinics with an intuitive, comprehensive, and secure digital management platform that streamlines operations, enhances patient care, and drives data-informed decision making.

Brand Values

Trust & Security
Patient data protection is paramount. Every feature is built with security first.
Simplicity
Complex healthcare workflows made intuitive and accessible for all staff levels.
Reliability
24/7 system availability. Clinics depend on us for uninterrupted operations.
Innovation
Continuously evolving with modern technology to serve healthcare better.

Target Audience

Medical clinics and healthcare facilities across Central Asia, ranging from small private practices to multi-location clinic networks. Primary users include clinic administrators, doctors, and medical staff.

02

Logo Guidelines

Shifo
Primary Logo
Used on light backgrounds. Heart icon paired with "Shifo" wordmark.
Shifo
Inverted Logo
Used on dark or colored backgrounds with white elements.
Icon Mark
Used for favicons, app icons, and compact spaces.

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the heart icon around all sides of the logo. Never crowd the logo with other elements.

Minimum Size

The full logo should never appear smaller than 120px wide. The icon mark should never be smaller than 24px.

Don'ts

Do not rotate the logo
Do not alter the colors
Do not stretch or distort
Do not add effects or shadows
Do not place on busy backgrounds
Do not rearrange elements

08

Iconography

Icon Library

Shifo uses the Lucide icon library (lucide-angular). Lucide provides consistent, clean, and modern line-based icons that align with the brand aesthetic.

Icon Sizing

Size Pixels Usage
xs 14px Inline with small text, badges
sm 16px Inline with body text, buttons
md 20px Navigation items, list icons
lg 24px Section headers, feature cards
xl 32px Feature showcases, empty states
2xl 48px Hero sections, branding

Icon Style Rules

Stroke Width
Default stroke width is 2px. Use 1.5px for large decorative icons (32px+).
Color
Icons inherit text color by default. Use brand colors for emphasis.
Alignment
Always vertically center icons with adjacent text. Use flexbox alignment.
Spacing
Maintain 8px minimum gap between icon and adjacent text elements.
09

Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

Shifo communicates with confidence and clarity. The tone is professional yet approachable, reflecting the seriousness of healthcare with the warmth of a trusted partner.

Principles

Clear
Use plain language. Avoid jargon. Technical terms are explained when necessary.
Confident
Speak with authority. The platform is reliable, and the language reflects that.
Caring
Remember the human element. Healthcare is personal, and our words show empathy.
Concise
Respect the reader's time. Say more with fewer words. Every word earns its place.

Multilingual Support

Shifo supports Russian and English as primary languages, with content localized for the Central Asian market. All UI text should be translation-ready and stored in structured translation objects. Right-to-left (RTL) support can be added as needed for future market expansion.
